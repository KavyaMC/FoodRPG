# Introduction

Welcome to Food RPG.

Food RPG is an accessible, menu-driven, audio-first role-playing game designed primarily for blind and screen reader users, while remaining enjoyable for anyone who prefers keyboard navigation and speech output.

The game is built around accessibility from the ground up rather than adding accessibility as an afterthought.

Food RPG focuses on:

- Audio-first gameplay
- Keyboard-only interaction
- Screen reader compatibility
- Simple, structured menus
- Clear spoken feedback

Throughout the game you will navigate menus, create your character, explore the world, interact with NPCs, manage your inventory, complete quests, and develop your character.
