# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 5, 2026

## Food RPG Privacy Policy

Food RPG respects your privacy.

The Software is designed to operate primarily on your local device. Unless expressly stated in a future release, Food RPG does not require the creation of an online account and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

The Software may create local files such as save data, settings, logs, and configuration files on your device. These files remain under your control and are used solely to support the operation of the Software.

If future versions introduce online features, cloud services, analytics, crash reporting, or account systems, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe the information collected, the purpose of collection, and how it is processed.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the Software.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information handled by the Software; however, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

By using Food RPG, you acknowledge and accept this Privacy Policy.
